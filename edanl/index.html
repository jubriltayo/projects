About Company

Who are we?

EngrDarwinA Nigeria Limited (EDANL) became registered as a Limited Liability Company on the 17th of October 2018 (RC-1532619). We got our first job of installing a 5.0 KVA Inverter at Cross River State in November 2018 and since then, we have taken up projects like Renovation of Ogoja Maternity Hospital, Monaiya, Installation of Sensor Automated Roller Gate, Installation of Electric fence wire and Barb Wires, Upgrade of Portacarbin etc.

This company was started up with the aim of FIXING IT ALL FOR YOU, with the zeal to connect you to a perfect solution provider for all problems within and outside the home, workplace, or construction site.
