About Us

Agrodextrous Services limited (RC: 1728803) is an engineering, design and fabrication company oriented towards the production of efficient, cost-effective, safe and easy-to-use material processing equipment and machinery. Since 2020, we have been dedicated to provide top-notch processing equipment, dependable services and solutions to operational challenges.

Within our 1,000-square-meter manufaturing facility located in Ahmadiiya, Lagos, Nigeria along with our state-of-the art equipment and our competent workforce we adopt modern engineering and manufacturing practices to reduce costs, boost efficiency without compromising on standards and quality of service delivered.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be Africa's best and leading agro-mechanical design and Fabrication Company with a focus on the development of postharvest processing-equipment.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to be a cutting-edge, value-driven, solutions-oriented, and innovative company that provides solutions to processing challenges encountered by farmers and agro-processors in Nigeria and Africa as a whole.

Core Values

Agrodextrous Services limited is a dedicated leader in the engineering design and fabrication industry, offering superior quality products and services that contribute to our customers' success. Our core values include:

  • Customer Focus - We ensure that our products meet our clients expectations at every stage in the product development, manufacture and support of our products. We continuously update and improve customer satisfaction and profitability.

  • Integrity and Professionalism - We strive to fulfill our responsibilities to the highest possible standards throughout.

  • Innovation - We are creative in delivering value to our fellow customers, associates, manufacturers and the community. We anticipate change and capitalize on opportunities that arise.

  • Accountability - We accept all our individuals and team resonsibilities and we meet our commitments. We take responsibility for our performace in all of our decisions and actions.

Customer Service Policy

Agrodextrous Services Limited is dedicated to providing first-rate customer service, with the goal of responding to our customers' needs in a timely manner. We strive to address any inquiry, question or concern within 24 hours of the initial communication via phone or e-mail. Requests for quotes are provided within three to five business days after all necessary information has been obtained. Additional time may be needed based on the complexity of the quote, availability of material pricing, or other information that may be required from a third-party vendor.
We aim to exceed all customer expectations by providing superior products that are delivered on time at a competitive price. Our experienced customer account managers are available to offer any needed assistance. Additional support may be provided by our compliance coordinator in seeking appropriate solutions.
Communication with our customers is vital to our success. As we place emphasis on exceptional customer satisfaction, we periodically initiate surveys to further evaluate our performance and identify any areas for possible improvement.
